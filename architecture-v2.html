⚡ AI PROACTIVE PREDICTION
The SLA engine doesn't just react — it predicts. If AI forecasts hub congestion on a route will cause a breach, it fires a YELLOW alert 12 hours before the threshold is actually hit. This gives ops teams time to reroute or expedite before the SLA is actually breached.
